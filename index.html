About Me


My name is Amelia Sanchez and I live in Abilene Texas. I first got interested with coding in high school, though I didn't decide to pursue it in college until after finishing my freshman year, I had always been interested in photography, graphic design, and computers and so I decided that this would be a worthwhile and fulfilling skill that I would enjoy learning. In 2019 I graduated from Abilene High and went on to pursue my associates of science at Cisco College. I decided to speed up my education and hone my coding skills so that I could start my coding career as soon as possible. I would like to one day become a software developer. Being a software developer interests me because it is never static work. I love the meticulous nature of coding and the different opportunities this field provides in regards to employment, creativity, and continuous learning.
